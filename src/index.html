About Big J's

Big J's Handyman Service is a family business operating throughout Indian River, Brevard, and St Lucie County. We provide a wide range of handyman and property maintenance services, helping you to improve the overall appearance and functionality of your home or office immediately!

Whether you're dealing with a high-stakes, multi-million-dollar project or simply repairing a couple of damaged wood pieces on your neighbor's house, we stand ready to surpass your every expectation, ensuring unparalleled quality.

We are now offering 24-hour emergency service due to water, wind, and fire damage.
